The Kate Shea Foundation is excited to announce that registration is open for the May 20, 2017, 14th Annual Bay Head 5k Memory Run! If you can not attend the race, but still want to support us, you can chose to donate an amount of your choice. This will benefit many great causes and will continue on the tradition of the annual run. We are incredibly proud to announce that because of your support last year, the Kate Shea Foundation donated a total of $76,500 dollars all of which went directly to research and education.

There are two options when making a donation, you can either donate to the Kate Shea Foundation or donate to a specific team or team member so that it goes towards their fundraising efforts.
