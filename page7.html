FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHEN ANS WHERE IS BIB PICK-UP?

BIB pick-up can be done between 5-7 pm at the Bay Head Firehouse ( 81 Bridge Avenue, Bay Head, NJ ) on Friday, May 18th or the morning of the race begining at 6:30 am. 

IF I AM SICK CAN SOMEONE RUN IN MY PLACE?

Unfortunately, no, we have bibs and shirts made prior to the race for the individual. We can not change names or shirts after registration. 

ARE DOGS ALLOWED?

Yes, dogs on a leash are allowed at the race.

CAN I ATTEND THE AFTER PARTY WITHOUT ATTENEDING THE RACE?

Yes! Go to Race Info and click on After Party to get your ticket. 

HOW DO I BECOME A SPONSOR?

HOW DO I BECOME A VOLUNTEER?
